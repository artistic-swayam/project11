Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer fabric pickup and delivery in Haldwani? +
Yes! For customers in Haldwani, we offer free fabric pickup and doorstep delivery for customised orders.
I’m outside Haldwani. Can I still order customised dresses? +
Absolutely! You can ship your fabric to us, and we’ll stitch your order as per your custom measurements. Once it’s ready, we’ll courier the final product back to your address.
What if I don’t have my own fabric? +
No worries. You can also choose from our pre-designed collection, or let us suggest fabrics for your customised outfit based on your preferences.
What is the cost structure? +
Our pricing for customised dresses includes stitching only. Fabric needs to be provided by the customer unless opting for pre-designed outfits. Additional customizations (like extra embroidery, linings, or design changes) may have extra charges.
How long does it take to receive the order? +
Custom orders usually take 5–10 working days, depending on complexity. Pre-designed dresses are shipped within 2–3 working days.
Do you offer returns or exchanges? +
Due to the personalised nature of custom clothing, we do not accept returns or exchanges on customised orders. However, if there’s a genuine issue with stitching or size, we’ll be happy to assist with free alterations (shipping charges may apply for non-local customers).
Can I track my order? +
Yes, once your product is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking ID via SMS or WhatsApp.
